Title: What is the Principle of Least Privilege?

The principle of least privilege defines that you should only allow a user access to information required to perform their job function. This is referenced as allowing information on a need-to-know basis.

Allowing a user access to more information than required to perform their job role could potentially compromise the confidentiality of the data.
