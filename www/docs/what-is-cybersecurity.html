Title: What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the process of securing physical and digital assets owned by a business or organization. In addition, cybersecurity is the process of developing a system by which authorized individuals may access assets to perform essential job functions, while preventing unauthorized individuals from accessing assets they do not have clearance to use. However, many people are under the impression that cybersecurity involves only the digital domain. Cybersecurity begins with the physical domain. We cannot begin to secure digital assets until we've established a secure environment for the physical domain.
